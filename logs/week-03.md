## Approach and Implementation

We shifted from three personas to a 2x2 design focused on explicit commitment and visual reinforcement.

For the commitment condition, participants would choose how many exercises they planned to complete. For visual reinforcement, Blossom could display a congratulatory ear-light sequence.

I began developing a Python script to generate numerical pattern-recognition tasks using categories such as even numbers, odd numbers, and prime numbers. Python controlled the task logic while Ollama handled the conversation.

I also compared numerical and alphabetical tasks and reviewed the participant interface and recruitment requirements.
